CRISPY TOFU STIR-FRY RECIPE

Crispy Tofu Stir-Fry Recipe

A simple and tasty vegan dish with crispy tofu and veggies.

Ingredients

  • ½ kilogram of tofu (tokwa), cubed
  • ½ kilogram of porkchop, cut into small pieces
  • ½ head of cabbage, chopped
  • 5 pieces of string beans (sitaw), cut into 2-inch pieces
  • 5 tomatoes, chopped
  • 3 cloves onions, minced
  • 1 sachet of Maggi Magic Sarap seasoning
  • 1 small can of Lucky 7 (corned beef or similar)
  • ½ teaspoon of salt
  • 5 pieces of chili green
  • 1 tablespoon ginger, grated
  • 3 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 3 tablespoons vegetable oil

Instructions

  1. Sauté the cubed tofu in a pan until golden brown.
  2. Remove from the pan and set aside.
  3. In the same pan, sauté the porkchop pieces until browned. Set aside.
  4. Sauté the onions, tomatoes, string beans, chili green, Lucky 7, and cabbage until the vegetables are cooked.
  5. Combine the cooked tofu and porkchop back into the pan with the sautéed vegetables.
  6. Add ½ teaspoon of salt and 1 sachet of Maggi Magic Sarap seasoning.
  7. Stir the mixture for a few minutes until well combined.
  8. Remove from heat and serve.
