My Family ♥

I grew up in a family full of love, perseverance, and support—where each member plays a unique role in shaping who I am today.

Mama

Mama is our strongest pillar, always working tirelessly and sacrificing her time for us. Whether selling goods in town or managing tasks at the office, she ensures that the three of us siblings are provided for and that our family stays together. Her love and dedication inspire us to persevere and value hard work.

Daddy

Daddy has another family with five children, but he continues to support and guide us siblings. Despite different households and situations, he has never neglected us. His presence and advice remind us of the importance of responsibility and family bonds.

Ate May

Ate May is married with four children—three boys and one girl—and she is the eldest among us siblings. Even though she has her own family, she continues to give us support, guidance, and love. She shows that family connections remain strong even with new responsibilities.

Jeselle

Jeselle has faced challenges since childhood, diagnosed with hearing impairment and speech delay due to a medication overdose. She uses a hearing aid and faces life with determination, courage, and resilience. She is an inspiration to us, teaching us to confront our own challenges with strength.

KC

I help Mama by selling and delivering goods in town or to offices. I am always busy assisting the family, ensuring everything runs smoothly, and supporting my siblings and Mama. Through my responsibilities, I have learned the values of perseverance, responsibility, and teamwork.

Kristine

Kristine is the youngest of our siblings, currently in Grade 8 in high school. She continues to grow and learn in a supportive family environment, and her presence brings joy and energy to our home.
